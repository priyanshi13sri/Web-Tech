TIGER

Characteristics

There are two recognized subspecies of tiger: the continental (Panthera tigris) and the Sunda.

Behavior & Habitat

Tigers are mostly solitary and mark their territory with scent and scratches. Sadly, they face threats like poaching, killings, and habitat loss due to human activity.

Conservation

Tigers are endangered but conservation efforts are ongoing. They are keystone species, vital for maintaining forest ecosystems.
